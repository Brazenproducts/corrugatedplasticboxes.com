Built for the Agricultural Supply Chain

Agricultural packaging faces conditions that destroy cardboard daily: field dust and moisture, rain during harvest, repeated loading from cold packing rooms to ambient trucks, and the mechanical stress of forklift handling across the supply chain.

PP corrugated agriculture boxes are engineered to handle all of it. UV stabilization prevents brittleness from sun exposure during field use. Waterproof construction performs through rain and cold storage. Vented wall designs keep produce fresh by maintaining airflow. And unlike cardboard — which is a write-off after every use — plastic boxes return on investment across 50+ cycles.

Elipacko manufactures PP corrugated agriculture boxes in any size, with custom venting patterns, colors for allergen or variety coding, and optional collapsible designs to minimize empty return volume. 0% import duty to the USA. USA manufacturing coming soon.
