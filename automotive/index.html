Why Automotive Supply Chains Choose PP Corrugated

Automotive parts logistics demands packaging that can survive hundreds of cycles through assembly plants, tier suppliers, and logistics providers without dimensional change. Cardboard compresses, warps, and fails — creating racking problems, part damage, and production line delays.

PP corrugated totes and boxes maintain exact dimensions across their entire service life. Closed-loop automotive packaging programs running 200–500 cycles per year depend on this dimensional consistency. The boxes stack to the same height on cycle one and cycle three hundred.

Elipacko manufactures automotive PP corrugated boxes with optional anti-static treatment for sensitive electronic components (ECUs, sensors, connectors), custom foam or divider inserts for part protection, and returnable packaging designs that fold flat to minimize empty return freight costs.
