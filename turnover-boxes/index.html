What Is a PP Corrugated Turnover Box?

A turnover box (also called a tote box, circulation box, or returnable transit packaging) is a reusable container designed to make multiple round trips between a supplier and a customer — or between different stations in a manufacturing facility — without being discarded.

PP corrugated turnover boxes are the lightweight, high-cycle alternative to injection-molded plastic crates. They offer most of the durability benefits of solid plastic at a fraction of the cost per unit, with the additional advantage of foldability in many designs for return-trip efficiency.

Three Turnover Box Configurations

Stackable Rigid

Standard fixed-wall construction. Stacks securely when loaded. Best for consistent routes where return volume isn't constrained. Maximum load rating available in heavy gauge PP.

Nestable

Tapered walls allow empty boxes to nest inside each other — reduces empty storage footprint without fold hardware. Best for high-traffic facilities with dedicated storage areas for empties.

Foldable / Collapsible

Walls fold down flat for return shipping and empty storage. 4:1 volume reduction vs. erected. Best for closed-loop programs where return freight cost is a significant variable.

With Lid

Snap-fit or hinged lid seals contents against dust, light, and contamination. Available in all three base configurations above. Food-grade and anti-static versions available.
