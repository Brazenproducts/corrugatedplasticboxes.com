Why Frozen Food Operations Choose PP Corrugated

Frozen food packaging is destroyed by the one thing it can't avoid: moisture. Cardboard absorbs condensation during freeze-thaw cycles and fails structurally — the main reason frozen food processors are switching to plastic at scale.

PP corrugated boxes are chemically impervious to moisture, ice, and the temperature cycling inherent to frozen food logistics. They hold their shape from blast freezer to delivery dock, maintain exact stacking dimensions in fully loaded cold storage, and can be pressure-washed and returned to service in minutes.

Elipacko's food-grade PP corrugated frozen food boxes are available in any dimension, with wall thicknesses from 4mm to 10mm, full-color printing, and custom venting or drain configurations. No distributor — factory direct pricing with 0% import duty to the USA.
